3.5.1 ELOG Alarm Code


ELOG-009 WARN call a service man

Cause: A system error has occurred.

Remedy: Perform a cold start: 1. Turn off the robot. 2. On the teach pendant, press and hold the SHIFT and RESET keys. 3. While still pressing the SHIFT and RESET keys, turn on the robot. If the error is not cleared, document the events that let to the error and call your FANUC Robotics technical representative.
